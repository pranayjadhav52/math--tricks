Math Tricks

Tricks

  • Multiplying by 11: Add the digits and put the sum in the middle (ex: 23 → 2+3=5 → 253).
  • Divisibility by 3: Add digits; if the sum is divisible by 3, so is the number.
  • Square ending in 5: (Ex: 25² = 625 → 2×(2+1)=6 → 625).

Examples

43 × 11 = 473
125² (ends in 5): 12 × 13 = 156 → 15625

Try It

Pick a 2-digit number and multiply it by 11. See the pattern!
